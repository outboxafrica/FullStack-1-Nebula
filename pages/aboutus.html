About Crisis Support Services of Kaama

Kaama Support Services of Uganda's mission is to provide 24/7, free, confidential and caring support to people in crisis. We work every day to be a beacon of hope in their darkest moments and empower them to see a better tomorrow. For more than 5 years, we have provided an empathetic ear, a caring heart and a helping hand to anyone in need.

We are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year to help anyone experiencing a personal crisis across the country. Last year, we helped more than 20,000 people through our hotline, text line and in-person advocacy services.
